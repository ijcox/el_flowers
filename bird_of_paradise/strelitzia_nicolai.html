Strelitzia nicolai – Giant White Bird of Paradise

Bird of Paradise nicolai is a large tropical species often resembling a palm-like tree with dramatic white flowers.

Description

Tall, clumping plant with banana-like leaves and large white-and-blue flowers emerging from dark bracts.

Etymology

Named after Grand Duke Nicholas of Russia (Nikolai Nikolaevich).

Taxonomy

Family: Strelitziaceae
Genus: Strelitzia
Species: S. nicolai

Distribution and Habitat

Native to coastal forests of South Africa and Mozambique.

Ecology

Pollinated by birds and large insects; forms dense tropical thickets in native habitat.

Cultivation

  • Full sun to partial shade
  • Well-drained soil
  • Requires space due to large size

Cultivars

  • Primarily wild form in cultivation

Symbolism

Represents grandeur, tropical majesty, and strength.
