Geranium 'Rozanne' – Hybrid Hardy Geranium

Geranium 'Rozanne' is a popular hybrid perennial prized for its long flowering season and striking blue-violet blooms.

Description

A vigorous, spreading perennial producing large violet-blue flowers with a white center from late spring to autumn.

Etymology

Named ‘Rozanne’ after Rozanne Waterer, associated with its development and introduction.

Taxonomy

Family: Geraniaceae
Genus: Geranium
Cultivar: ‘Rozanne’

Distribution and Habitat

A cultivated hybrid widely grown in temperate gardens around the world.

Ecology

Highly attractive to bees and pollinators due to its long flowering period.

Cultivation

  • Full sun to partial shade
  • Well-drained soil
  • Low maintenance perennial
  • Excellent ground cover

Cultivars

  • ‘Rozanne’ – award-winning long bloomer

Symbolism

Represents endurance, elegance, and continuous bloom.
